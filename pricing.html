Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my minutes?

Additional minutes are charged at $0.10 per minute. You can upgrade your plan anytime to get more included minutes at a better rate.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees on any plan. You can start using our service immediately after signing up.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers for annual plans, and can arrange invoicing for Enterprise customers.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What's included in support?

All plans include access to our knowledge base. Higher tiers get priority support via email, chat, and phone with guaranteed response times.
